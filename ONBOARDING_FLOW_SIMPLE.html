🚀 Onboarding Flow — 5 Steps

🔵 HashVault
1
Chargement App Wallet présumé connecté
Route: / (unique)
État: Wallet déjà affiché (0x7Ab…a3F2)
Visible: Dashboard complet avec 3 positions
Navigation: 3 tabs (Dashboard, My Vaults, Invest)
↓
2
Dashboard
Tab interne (pas de routing)
KPIs: Portfolio value, Yield earned
Charts: Line chart 12 mois
Table: Vos 3 vaults avec progress
↓
3
My Vaults
Tab interne
Détails: 3 vault cards complètes
Strategy: Scenarios interactifs
Transactions: History expand/collapse
↓
4
Invest → Select
Tab interne + Stepper Step 1
Choix: 2 vaults (Prime / Growth)
Infos: APY, Lock, Min deposit, Risk
CTA: "Select →"
↓
5
Invest → Deposit
Stepper Step 2-3-4
Product: Key terms + Strategy donut
Amount: Input USDC + Scenarios chart
Confirm: Success screen
🟢 Hearst Connect
1
Landing Page Public
Route: /
Hero: Turn Bitcoin Mining Into Structured Yield
Sections: Intro, Features, Solutions, Who
CTA: "Launch App" (header) → /vaults
↓
2
Vaults Gallery Public
Route: /vaults
Header: Bouton "Connect"
Vaults: Cards clickables
Modal: Détails vault + CTA selon état wallet
↓
3
Connect Wallet Action requise
Modal ou Header button
États gérés:
• Non connecté → "Connect Wallet"
• Wrong chain → "Switch to Base"
• Connecté + Base → Accès OK
↓
4
Enter Platform Protected

Si OK: Canvas interface
↓
5
Portfolio Canvas Protected
Route: /app (Canvas component)
Vue: Positions existantes
Navigation: Vault selection interne
Actions: Subscribe, manage positions

📊 Résumé des différences

HashVault:
• Aucun onboarding (direct app)
• Wallet présumé connecté
• SPA (tabs internes)
• Stepper invest visible
Hearst Connect:
• Progressive disclosure (Landing → Vaults → App)
• Connect wallet flow explicite
• Multi-page routing
• Access gates sur /app
